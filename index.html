Established in 2025, Feltham Fun Holiday Club was created with a mission to provide children with a fun, active, and imaginative alternative to screen time during school breaks.

Whether it's arts and crafts, group challenges, storytelling, or hands-on discovery, every day is packed with opportunities for social interaction and cognitive growth.

Our experienced team creates a warm, inclusive environment where children of all backgrounds feel safe, supported, and inspired to explore. We focus on collaboration, imagination, and play â€” the foundations of lifelong learning and confident communication.
